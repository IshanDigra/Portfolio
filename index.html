About Me

A little bit about my background and professional journey.

I grew up in a small village near Pathankot, Punjab, where life was simple and the biggest traffic jam was usually cows on the road. From there, life took me to IIT Delhi - my second home, where I studied Electrical Engineering, and that’s where I found my love for coding.

Since then, I’ve been building and improving backend systems, first at Jio and now at Goldman Sachs. What I enjoy most is solving problems in a way that makes life easier for people using the products.
