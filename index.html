About

Hey there! I’m Ishan Digra, currently a Junior Backend Developer at Jio Platforms Limited, right here in vibrant Koparkhairane, Navi Mumbai. But let’s rewind a bit. I hail from the quaint village of Pathankot, Punjab, where the only traffic jam we ever faced was a herd of cows leisurely crossing the road. Most of my formative years were spent in hostels, away from home, which means I’ve perfected the art of living out of a suitcase and surviving on instant noodles. My journey into the tech world began during college when I was struck by a lightning bolt of passion for software development. An SDE internship at Huawei in my third year was like receiving my Hogwarts letter—my ticket to the realm of software development.

Full Stack Developer
